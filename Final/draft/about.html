Description of Game

End of Magic is the working title for White Rapidâ€™s upcoming debut release. It is an action-platformer mixed with elements of the fighting genre and set on a stage filled with magic, monsters and plenty of characters to interact with.

Magic once prevailed. Many centuries ago, almost everyone could use magic, and even those who were the least skilled could still make use of the abilities gained from it. Now, magic is dead. Those who can still use it are looked upon as little more than monsters, and so most of them hide their abilities from the world.

There are some, however, who will take advantage of their power to assert dominion over others. They are mostly small threats, having only a slight grasp on how to use their ability. However, a truly evil entity will soon discover that it can wield powerful magic, and the entire world will be threatened.

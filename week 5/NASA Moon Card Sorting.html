Results from card sorting site Optimal Workshop

After creating the cards on the Optimal Workshop website I performed the card sort my self as well as watch 3 other individuals perform the same card sort. I noticed that people would often create initial groups and them move card around as they decided names for the groups.

After the card sorting was finished, the website I used for card sorting displayed graphs showing common groupings among the particpants as well as proposed a sample grouping that was based on the responses. Our Information Architecture matched the NASA website on the groupings of Moon Information and Missions. Besides that the architecture did was different from our groupings.

To Do List Card Sorting

Basic Features

  • Add Task
  • Ending Soonest
  • Remove Task
  • Overview
  • Completed Events
  • Manage Tasks
  • Notifications
  • Reminders

Advance Features

  • Calendar
  • Share Task
  • Search
  • Priorities
  • Email Integrations
  • Twitter Integrations
  • Facebook Integrations
  • Voice Commands
  • Audio Recording

After creating the cards on the Optimal Workshop website I performed the card sort my self as well as watch 3 other individuals perform the same card sort. I noticed that people would often create initial groups and them move card around as they decided names for the groups.

After the card sorting was finished, the website I used for card sorting displayed graphs showing common groupings among the participants as well as proposed a sample grouping that was based on the responses.

Most participants sorted their cards based on the amount of use that each feature would be of use to them. I structured my sort based on two categories: basic and advanced features.
